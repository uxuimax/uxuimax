Syllabus

Week 1 🌟

We start each day with a 40-minute design sprint. This week, we’ll go through the complete curriculum from the UXUIMAX free course, focusing on User Experience. We’ll conduct interviews with real users and learn how to leverage AI for extensive research. Together, we’ll design a mobile app for both Android and iOS devices.

Week 2 🎛️

Daily design sprints continue, but now we’ll also reflect on last week’s work. This week’s focus is on Design Language—a methodical approach to communicating with your audience through product design. Topics include elevation, motion, visual design, and creating guidelines. During the week, we’ll collaborate on a web app SaaS project.

Week 3 🗄️

The spotlight this week is on Core Components—mastering every pixel and building reusable component libraries. These principles will be applied to design sprint projects and to a new project for the week: a VR app for visionOS! We’ll also revisit previous design sprints and projects to establish quality maintenance.

Week 4 🥇

Please don’t cry—this is the final week! We’ll focus entirely on design sprints while delving into the psychology of design. As we perfect three major projects from previous weeks for your Case Studies, we’ll discuss strategies for interviews and presenting your work effectively, whether for a job opportunity or a client proposal. I’ll also share personal insights and tips on freelancing to help you succeed.
